Finance Department Internal Documentation

Reimbursement Process:
1. Log into finance portal
2. Submit expense report with receipts
3. Manager approval required
4. Reimbursement processed within 2 weeks
5. Direct deposit to your account

Budget Reports Location:
- Monthly reports: Finance Portal > Reports > Monthly
- Quarterly summaries: SharePoint > Finance > Q Reports
- Annual budgets: Shared Drive > Finance > Annual

Payroll Schedule:
- Processed on the 25th of every month
- If 25th is weekend/holiday, processed on previous business day
- Direct deposit by end of business day
- Pay stubs available on HR portal

Expense Categories:
- Travel: Flights, hotels, transportation
- Meals: Client dinners, team lunches
- Office Supplies: Stationery, equipment
- Professional Development: Courses, conferences
